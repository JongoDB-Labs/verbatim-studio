User Research — Interview Notes

Participant: P03 — Senior PM at fintech startup
Date: October 2026
Format: 30-min remote interview

Key findings:

1. Privacy is the primary purchase motivator. P03 ruled out Otter and Fireflies because of cloud-only architecture. "My CFO transcripts can't leave my laptop."

2. Existing tools fail on domain vocabulary. P03 cited consistent misrecognition of company-specific acronyms — they switch to manual editing 5-10 times per transcript.

3. Project-based organization is critical. P03 manages 8 ongoing client engagements; needs each transcript / document / note tied to a specific client folder.

4. Voice search > keyboard search. P03 prefers asking the assistant a question over crafting search queries. "What did Sarah say about the pricing model?" feels more natural than "sarah pricing model."

Action items:
- Verify our vocabulary correction handles their acronym list
- Confirm project isolation is intuitive in onboarding
- Test voice chat with Max on multi-speaker transcripts
